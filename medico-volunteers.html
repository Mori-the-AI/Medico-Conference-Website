Volunteer FAQs

Questions answered.

Any student currently enrolled at Bowen University is eligible to apply. We particularly welcome students from clinical and basic science years. External applicants may also be considered for selected units at the committee's discretion.

You may express interest in a second unit in your application form. However, we strongly recommend identifying your primary unit preference and committing fully — each unit requires genuine dedication, and split attention affects team performance.

Volunteers are expected to attend at least two pre-conference planning meetings with their unit, a full team rehearsal on the eve of the conference, and be present for the full duration of the conference day. Total commitment is approximately 10–14 hours across the period.

All confirmed volunteers receive: a branded Medic'o volunteer t-shirt, a certificate of participation signed by the conference chair and Bowen University faculty, full delegate access to keynote sessions, lunch and refreshments, and priority access to future Medic'o events.

All applications are reviewed by the respective unit heads. You will receive a response within 5 working days of the application deadline. Shortlisted volunteers may be called for a brief informal conversation with the unit head before confirmation.
