About the Conference

Where the next generation of healthcare leadership is forged.

Medic'o Conference is Bowen University's flagship annual medical conference — a space where students, clinicians, researchers, and policymakers converge to push the boundaries of what's possible in African healthcare.

Each edition is anchored by a defining theme. In 2025, we gather under F.O.R.G.E. — a call to action for every medical professional to step into the frontier, challenge convention, and build something worth inheriting.

Join Us in 2025

The Theme Unpacked

Tap each letter to explore what it means.

F
Frontiers

Pushing the edge of what medical science knows and does — from diagnostics to delivery systems, from genomics to grassroots care. The frontier is wherever the unresolved still lives.

O
Of

"Of" is the bridge — the connector that binds ambition to action. It reminds us that growth and excellence are not standalone achievements; they are always built of something, in service of something greater.

R
Revolutionary

Not incremental — paradigm-shifting. Revolutionary healthcare thinking challenges assumptions about how medicine is taught, delivered, funded, and experienced, especially in under-resourced contexts like ours.

G
Growth

Personal, professional, and systemic growth. From the first-year student to the entire healthcare ecosystem — we are all under construction. Medic'o is where that construction is celebrated and accelerated.

E
Excellence in Healthcare

A non-negotiable standard — precision, compassion, and innovation in every clinical encounter. Excellence is not a destination; it is a discipline practiced daily in the ward, the lab, and the lecture hall.
